# Compliance Standard

## Obrigatorio

- Identify personal and sensitive data in specs and data models.
- Apply LGPD considerations when personal data exists.
- Define audit requirements for critical operations.
- Document retention or deletion requirements when they affect the domain.

## Recomendado

- Prefer data minimization in forms, APIs, and persistence.
- Keep compliance decisions visible in `_appgen_work/decisions.md`.

## Proibido

- Collect sensitive data without a documented business reason.
- Expose personal data in logs, errors, or default list responses.
